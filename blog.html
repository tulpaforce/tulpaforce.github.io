Tulpaforce Me

10/6/2013

0 Comments

 
    As of today, I started constructing TulpaForce.github.io. I originally intended to construct a new community website, but due to incompetent service teams that support certain community software, I decided I would make a more static website - because what else am I going to do with this domain I paid for?

      I picked the name after much thought. Initially I considered tulpamancer.com, but decided against it, since it sounded too esoteric. Then, an inside joke from the #tulpa.info channel on Rizon came to mind: tulpacalypse.com. Nobody would have gotten it though. Finally, I imagined a tulpa pouting, yelling at her host, "tulpaforce me!" - and before I knew it I dropped $9 on a new domain. Hopefully the image of the stunning Kousaka Kirino alongside the website name will entice tulpamancers to force their own tulpas using my guides and resources.
