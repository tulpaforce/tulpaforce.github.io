Activities

    Here's a list of possible things to do with your tulpa, in case you're bored and don't know what to do. Some of these can be done in the wonderland, or in real life.

  • Play a video game.
  • Watch a movie.
  • Read a book or graphic novel.
  • Have a staring contest.
  • Buy a hamburger.
  • Go on a walk.
  • Swim.
  • Cook a meal.
  • Build a fort.
  • Draw pictures in total darkness.
  • Put on socks and run and slide on a hard wood floor.
  • Water balloon fight.
  • Ice skating.
  • Listen to music.
  • Talk with an accent.
  • Learn how to make ice cream.
  • Have a concert.
  • Watch TV.
  • Hit the random button on Wikipedia.
  • Breathe on a window and draw on it.
  • Practice possession.
  • Draw on yourselves.
  • Go bowling.
  • Play Civilization.
  • Walk around a shopping mall.
  • Let your tulpa paint your nails.
  • Exercise.
  • Write a song about tulpas.
  • Spy on your neighbors.
  • Do homework.
  • Ride a bike.
  • Try on clothes.
  • Have a marble tournament.
  • Go to an amusement park.
  • Dance.
  • Have a picnic.
  • Argue with someone on the internet.
  • Have a rap battle.
  • Blow bubbles.
  • Learn how to use chopsticks.
  • Learn Morse code.
  • Do laundry or other chores.
  • Let your tulpa chat with someone on Omegle
  • Make the ultimate sandwich
  • Throw a party, with balloons.
  • Play chess.
  • Braid your tulpa's hair.
  • Water your tulpa to see if she grows.
  • Play basketball.
  • Pretend you can do parkour.
  • Seduce your tulpa.
  • Go to McDonalds and pretend you can't speak English.
  • Debate politics.
  • Devise how you would rob a store and get away.
  • Meditate.
  • Go on reddit.
  • Have a snowball fight.
  • Go to a water park.
  • Engage in hand to hand combat.
  • Go-kart race.
  • Acquire 100 slinkies, tangle them up to make a slinky-ball.
  • Play a trading card game like Yu-Gi-Oh or Magic the Gathering.
  • Eat a habanero chili.
  • Talk about your feelings on the internet.
  • Let your tulpa play an MMORPG.
  • Rave.
  • Build stuff out of Legos.
